One Scan. The Whole Room.

The QR scanner is the engine. The same motion recognizes davening, learning, behavior, and more — no menus, no mode-switching, no separate activity to run.

Recognize

Pick what you're looking for, then scan. Good davening, Derech Eretz, helping a chavrusa — caught the moment it happens.

Track

Attendance, homework, chazan, test grades — one Gradebook, filled in by the same scan you already make.

Learn

Pesukim and Mishnayos with phrase-by-phrase translation, matching games, and quizzes — pulled from your own text.

Reward

A prize store, a raffle wheel, a Chinese auction — real motivation, with every prize tracked until it's handed out.

Own Your Data

Everything saves to your own Google Sheet and your own device. No accounts, no lock-in, works offline.

No Computer in the Room? No Problem.

Some rebbeim don't want a laptop or tablet open while they teach — and they shouldn't have to. Menchmark works with your scanner's own built-in memory: scan students off their printed pages all day long, with nothing running in the classroom at all.

At the end of the day, hand the scanner to your secretary — or just bring it home. One bulk upload sends the entire day's scans into Menchmark at once — each one time-stamped and in order, exactly as it happened.

1

Scan all day, offline. The scanner stores every scan in its own memory — no phone, no computer, no internet.

2

Hand it off. Give the scanner to your secretary, or plug it into your home computer that evening — nothing for you to type up.

3

Bulk upload. One click loads the whole day in — review it, then commit. Every scan lands with its real time.

How It Works

Up and running in under 2 minutes

Four steps from zero to scanning your first Mensch.

1

Copy the Apps Script

Open the template link, deploy it as a web app, and copy the URL.

2

Paste your URL

Go to Settings in Menchmark and paste the script URL. Test the connection.

3

Enter student names

Type or paste your roster. QR codes are generated automatically.

4

Start marking Mensches!

Scan, tap, and award. The leaderboard updates across every device instantly.

Why we built this

Made by rebbeim, for the way you already teach.

Menchmark started in a real classroom, not a boardroom. Most classroom apps ask you to stop teaching and go run an activity — open a screen, click through menus, manage a system. That's backwards. A rebbi teaches naturally, and the good moments happen on their own: a boy helps his chavrusa, davens with kavanah, holds the door without being asked.

We wanted a tool that just catches those moments and quietly keeps track — one scan, right in the flow of the shiur, with the character traits our mesorah actually cares about at the center. Not points for their own sake. Middos and Derech Eretz, made visible.

So we built it, used it with our own talmidim, and kept it honest: it works on your own device, saves to your own Google Sheet, needs no student logins, and runs even when the internet doesn't. It's free and open source, and it always will be — because it was built to serve rebbeim, not to sell them anything.
