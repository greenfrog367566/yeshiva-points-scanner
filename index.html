Rebbi Onboarding Guide

Up and running in under 2 minutes

Follow these steps to connect your classroom hardware with your cloud-free database.

Open the setup configuration in the app's Settings → Google Sheets panel. Deploy the spreadsheet logic via Apps Script as a Web App. Paste that secure link back into your dashboard—your sheet is now configured to aggregate points data across your entire grade.
Customize your point distribution menus. Set up dedicated triggers for Learning targets, Tefillah milestones, and custom Middos goals tailored precisely to your classroom's daily requirements.
Input or paste your class roster in the settings card. The platform instantly draws clean, uniquely serialized vector QR tokens for each student. Print them to a standard sheet of sticker paper or cardstock.
Connect your USB or paired Bluetooth handheld scanner to your desktop or laptop. Open the Scan Dashboard, tap a point amount, and squeeze the scanner trigger over a student's card. Data handles instantly without disrupting your instruction.

Why Menchmark?

Built for Rebbeim, by Rebbeim

Universal Scanner Compatibility

Acts as a standard keyboard emulator. Works natively with any off-the-shelf USB barcode scanner or wireless Bluetooth scanner wand—no proprietary hardware required.

Multi-Rebbi Cloud Sync

Because your Google Sheet aggregates logs dynamically, multiple Rebbeim, Moros, or Menahalim can record tracking changes simultaneously from separate workstations.

Torah & Tefillah Tracking

Optimize data logging for study review cycles. Quickly switch tracking profiles to record oral review updates, Mishnayos lines, or Tefillah milestones.

Middos-Focused Metric Engine

Easily reward good middos across your class. Instantly record Tefillah Bikavana, finger on the place, or classroom participation with a single click.

Open Source & Community-Driven

Menchmark is distributed entirely open source. Any developer, administrator, or tech-forward educator can review the file safety, deploy standalone variations, or adapt it to their institution's unique learning criteria.
