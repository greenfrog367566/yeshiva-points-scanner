How It Works

Up and running in under 2 minutes

Four steps from zero to a live classroom economy.

1

Copy the Apps Script

Open the template link, deploy it as a web app, and copy the URL.

2

Paste your URL

Go to Settings in Menchmark and paste the script URL. Test the connection.

3

Enter student names

Type or paste your roster. QR codes are generated automatically.

4

Start marking Mensches!

Scan, tap, and award. The leaderboard updates across every device instantly.
