Menchmark is almost ready.

Menchmark was built by a rebbi who spent almost a decade trying everything — raffle tickets, point charts, post-its, clipboards, ClassDojo-style apps — across real classrooms. Each one got something right; none of them got it all. Menchmark takes the best of each and puts it in one tool. We're inviting a small group of rebbeim to try it first, and we'd love for you to be one of them.
